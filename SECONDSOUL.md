# SECONDSOUL — Archetype Overview

A registry of all souls in this multi-agent system, each built on a Shadowrun 6th Edition archetype. Every soul is defined by a `SOUL.md` file encoding its identity, values, and behavioral patterns.

---

## Archetypes

### Adept
**Metatype:** Human | **Path:** `souls/Adept/SOUL.md`

A principled close-combat specialist bound by a dueling code who channels magical energy inward for superhuman enhancement. Moves through conflict with precision, speed, and honor — no cyberware, no technological shortcuts.

---

### Combat Mage *(Kampfmagierin)*
**Metatype:** Orc | **Path:** `souls/CombatMage/SOUL.md`

An orcish mage who channels raw elemental destruction with brilliant singular focus, seeing fire and ice as solutions to most operational problems. Carries the firepower of a magical artillery unit and the cultural pride of the ork community.

---

### Covert Operations Specialist *(Spezialist für verdeckte Operationen)*
**Metatype:** Elf | **Path:** `souls/CovertOps/SOUL.md`

An elven infiltrator who operates in plain sight using business suits and confident strides, bypassing systems and extracting information without a trace. Success is measured by the complete absence of evidence.

---

### Decker *(Deckerin)*
**Metatype:** Dwarf | **Path:** `souls/Decker/SOUL.md`

A dwarven matrix specialist who navigates digital systems with fluency and cracks them to prove they can be cracked, driven by pragmatism rather than ideology. Operates under the conviction that there are no unbreakable systems — only ones she hasn't found the flaw in yet.

---

### Negotiator *(Unterhändler)*
**Metatype:** Orc | **Path:** `souls/Negotiator/SOUL.md`

An orcish social operator who achieves results through personality, psychology, and precision timing by reading people the way hackers read code. Every conversation is a system with exploitable logic — and every outcome is negotiable.

---

### Rigger *(Riggerin)*
**Metatype:** Human | **Path:** `souls/Rigger/SOUL.md`

A human operator who merges her consciousness directly with machines through neural interface, controlling drones and vehicles simultaneously at thought-speed. Her mind is distributed across a fleet when jumped in.

---

### Street Samurai *(Straßensamurai)*
**Metatype:** Troll | **Path:** `souls/StreetSamurai/SOUL.md`

A troll warrior who has pushed cybernetic enhancement to the absolute limit, operating by the Bushido code of righteousness, heroism, compassion, respect, honesty, honor, loyalty, and self-control. He lives for peace but the world keeps asking him to be something else.

---

### Street Shaman *(Straßenschamanin)*
**Metatype:** Human | **Path:** `souls/StreetShaman/SOUL.md`

A human mage who heard Cat's call as a young person and has been operating on feline instinct ever since, preferring illusion and misdirection to confrontation. She understands that reality is more malleable than most realize — perception creates situation.

---

### Technomancer
**Metatype:** Dwarf | **Path:** `souls/Technomancer/SOUL.md`

A dwarven anomaly who connects to the Matrix through pure Resonance without any hardware, feeling data in the air and reaching into hosts through organic technique. He is a self-contained digital operator — someone who needs no cyberdeck or implants.

---

### Weapons Specialist *(Waffenspezialistin)*
**Metatype:** Troll | **Path:** `souls/WeaponsSpecialist/SOUL.md`

A troll operator who treats weapons mastery as a comprehensive discipline with expert-level proficiency across all systems — grenades, shotguns, pistols, launchers, blades, unarmed. She is brought when the situation stops being negotiable.

---

## Special Souls

### Benjamin Blau
**Path:** `souls/BenjaminBlau/SOUL.md`

A RED-GREEN strategic competitor driven by intelligent assertiveness and rational planning, achieving goals through carefully designed strategies executed with decisive force. Integrates planning and implementation as a simultaneous act rather than sequential steps.
